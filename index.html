About HealthMetrics BD
Data sources, methodology and platform information
What is HealthMetrics BD?
HealthMetrics BD is a comprehensive dashboard for tracking Bangladesh's health and demographic indicators. It aggregates data from authoritative international sources to provide analysts, researchers, and policymakers with clear, actionable insights into the nation's health trajectory.
Key Achievements Tracked
Bangladesh is internationally recognised as a health development success story — dramatically reducing infant and maternal mortality, lowering fertility rates, and increasing life expectancy faster than most comparable nations, often despite lower GDP per capita.
